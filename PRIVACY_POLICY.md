# Privacy Policy for AutoUploader Studio

**Last Updated: February 20, 2026**

AutoUploader Studio ("we," "us," or "our") operates this Meta Application. This policy informs you of our practices regarding data collection.

### 1. Data Collection
We do not store personal user data. We only access your Instagram Business Account ID and media files via Meta APIs to perform uploads as requested by the user.

### 2. Data Usage
The accessed data (video URL and account ID) is used solely to facilitate the publishing of content to your connected Instagram Business accounts.

### 3. Data Deletion
Users can revoke access at any time through their Facebook Business Integrations. To request manual deletion of any session logs, contact: andrewbonnguyen@gmail.com.
